Grandma's Secret Lasagna Recipe

straight from the old country, filled with cheese and love. this authentic recipe will bring joy to your home and and smiles to the face of any and all.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Boil Noodles for 8min
  2. Drain and rince noodles
  3. Layer Noodles, Cheese, and Sauce in 9x13 cassarole dish
  4. Bake at 375 for 30 min
